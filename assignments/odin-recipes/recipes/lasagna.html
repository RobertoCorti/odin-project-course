Lasagne

Description

Delight in layers of tender lasagna noodles, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. This homemade lasagna is a comforting masterpiece, where flavors meld and textures harmonize, creating a culinary experience that satisfies and indulges. Gather your loved ones and savor the artistry of Italian cuisine in every delicious bite.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until they are softened and lightly browned.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until it is browned and cooked through. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend together.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, and beaten egg. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle a third of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
  10. Repeat the layers with half of the remaining meat sauce, 3 more noodles, the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and another third of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  11. Place the final 3 lasagna noodles on top and cover them with the remaining meat sauce.
  12. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese on top, and if desired, sprinkle a little more grated Parmesan cheese.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure it is tightly sealed.
  14. Bake in the preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  16. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  17. Slice the lasagna into portions and serve hot. Enjoy!
