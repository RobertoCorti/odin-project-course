Parmigiana di Melanzane

Description

Parmigiana di Melanzane, also known as Eggplant Parmesan, is a beloved Italian dish. Slices of eggplant are fried to perfection and layered with rich tomato sauce and a combination of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Baked until golden and bubbly, each bite offers a delightful blend of textures and flavors. It's a comforting and satisfying dish that captures the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants
  • 2 cups tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the eggplants into 1/4-inch thick rounds.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  4. Dip each eggplant slice into the beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry the eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides, working in batches if needed. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom.
  8. Place a layer of fried eggplant slices on top of the sauce.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the eggplant.
  10. Repeat the layers of sauce, eggplant, and cheese until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  13. Garnish with chopped fresh basil.
  14. Serve warm and enjoy!
